    A word of advice for you all

    Dear freshmen,
    Congratulations to everyone who is about to graduate from high school. First of all, I welcome you to enter the warm, friendly and harmonious family of Fu Jen Catholic University. I think this is an entirely correct choice. Fu Jen Catholic University is pleased to have the honor of spending the best chapter of life with you. I want to encourage you, “Dream what you want to dream; go where you want to go; be what you want to be, because you have only one life and one chance to do all the things you want to do." I look forward to seeing you.
